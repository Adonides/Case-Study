Who am I?

By Adonides Amparo
April 21st, 2021

For the connection I would love to have with my readers, I believe we all should present ourselves to create a deep bond for a heath friendship.

Hello, I am Adonides Amparo, bachelor’s in business management in Dublin, Ireland 2018, current study coding and Spanish by myself.

First of all, I would have presented myself before any article in this blog, but as we are living a quarantine, one pandemic situation and the blog’s public to be today just family and close friends, my priority was pass what is the changing in this world straight, writing in the past 3 weeks to expend the information’s about this theme.

I was born in São Paulo, Brazil, I am 33-year-olds, graduated in a public high school in Brazil, I always liked to learn new things. I attended several technical courses including Spanish and English in my teens, courses such as computer hardware, industrial mechanic, forklift operator, warehouse management to Logistics. I have been chasing knowledge in a general and non-specific way.

When I was 24, I decided to go abroad to get the fluency in English, I spent 2 years studying English to go to university in Dublin, I lived in Ireland for 6 years between studying, travelling (what I love to do) and working as a forklift driver.

I moved out to Portugal in 2018, with the feeling of adventure to explore new opportunities, I had couple of sub jobs, but the one I stay longer was the waiter. The country is amazing, beautiful nature I made sure to explore that as well. I quit the job in the middle of the pandemic in September last year.

In November 2020 I started to be a self-taught web developer and I am current studying HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node JS. I am in love with technology as it is something we never stop to learn. It suits me as I like to learn, and this market is a great challenge. In February, the idea of this blog came on board and after I return to Brazil recently in March to spend this time with my family, I put the blog online.

My main characteristic is I am open to discussion and I love that!!! I believe it is one of the best ways to learn, to be open for other opinion and absorb good critics. I know my qualities and where I need to improve always learn is my way to live!
