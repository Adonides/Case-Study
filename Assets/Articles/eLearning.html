You should be picky with E-learning!

By Adonides Amparo
April 07th, 2021

There are so many online course providers nowadays. Long- or short-term courses, paid or free courses, all courses are a serious investment of time!

Online learning has been growing in the last years, but because the pandemic it is got a boom, the situation pushed everyone into online and the school’s businesses was forced to launch platforms online to sell them content to keep the business running, but all this with not much time to plan, the level of online education that was not excellent fell drastic more with the high demand.

In the other side, the student of traditional education was forced as well to attend e-learning, as most of these students had never taken an online course or had taken one but dropped out the course for any reason. It has been hard for everyone because they are not used to it and for anything, we need adaptation.

There are many difficulties for this adaptation. With all difficulties school’s business had to find the way, now with more difficulties come the students with less money or skills, for some of them almost impossible. There are students whose do not know to use the compute, and there are some whose do not have the right structure to study at home.

It is well known to anyone that there are bad service in any business, online courses are not different. Many e-learning can be bad with just a tone of text to read or with a difficult platform to navigate, keep in mind we should never be generalist and see the big picture. We are constantly changing, and the education must improve to keep up with our changing, we must always demand the best!

The main difficulties people say about online course, it's about to be lonely, that students want to be around to other students and because of this lack of talking, it is boring. It is not true, we are able to find an online course dynamic, fun and interactive, there are many providers offering all types of different online learning, with videos, challenges games, stories etc. This is on you to know yourself to pick the right course for you, and we have more time to be with friends and family with e-learning remember it!

We are looking for new skills and develop our professional career for that we must accept these challenges and believe in our potential in all path we choose to achieve them, and trough online course could be a good way to us to start it or even to be our main resource to conquer it.
