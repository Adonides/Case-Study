Online Education might be for you!

By Adonides Amparo
April 28th, 2021

You will be surprised how far you can go with e-learning, but which characteristics should have an online student?

I have heard that exist one part of people who do not have the characterises to study online, in my mind it is not exactly true, because the real reason just might be they are not ready or adapted for it yet.

If you are into any of these characteristics below, it means online education might be for you:

Familiar with social media

There is a great chance that you might adapt to study online if you like interaction on social media, this is the main interaction with the colleagues of the online courses, which could be for WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and of course the online platform provided by the institution of the course. It does not mean you cannot invite your classmates from the online course to go out in person, quite the opposite, it is health and I recommend it if you have this possibility.

Technology has become part of our life, especially our mobile, which is very personal, where we keep our details and privacy. In social media we discuss and discuss with family and friends, in an online course you will discuss and learn from your colleagues and lectures also online.

In case you are doing a course alone in one platform online, you always can go to different communities on the social media, for example if you are doing an English course there are many groups on Facebook where people are study English in different levels who help each other.

Desire to challenge yourself

Studies online develops more self-learn inside the students. We all are self-learners no matter method of education we choose between traditional or online and knowing as well how important self-learn is for our life to be active learners, I mean to go after the knowledge, it is the fast way learn.

It also develops your organization and time management, with a list of tasks and to follow it strictly, teaching you to work better with deadlines and to improve self-discipline.

Managing the time, we can find better solutions to conciliate work, family, friends and studying. Nowadays this flexibility is one the main reason online courses as growing so much the last years, many people do not have other way to study.

Independence

If you do not like to depend on other people, but you like to deal with different situations, running after the problem, and do not let people do it for you. Online course is for you, with e-learning you must do research more often to get information and thus learn for yourself. However, you still have your colleagues and lectures online or social media communities to learn more about the subject.

Self Confidence

Usually, self-confidence people like to act, without waiting for others and always try to do the best they can in any task they have to do. Those people are self-motivated as well, what make easy to them keep going even into some difficulties.
