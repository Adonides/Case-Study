Pros of Enrolling in Traditional Education

By Adonides Amparo
April 14th, 2021

When we should choose the traditional education as the method of learning, analysing all the components.

Traditional education has been the most chosen by students in their first graduation course, however this number has been decreased year by year. It is especially important to say that there must be a connection between who we are, our characteristics, the career we want to pursue and more the characteristics of the method to choose.

Interaction and Network

A close relation with lectures and other students, are good for a dynamic exchange of knowledge between colleagues, where in my experience is when you learn more, and the ease of taking your doubts face to face with the lecture. You can get knowledge faster with brainstorms (different points of views) as well as the motivation to be surround of people who want to achieve the same goal.

We can build a faster network with face-to-face contact from the first week of classes and this can have an immersed value for the professional career, with a closer relationship the colleagues start to have a trusting relationship becoming a co-worker or even become a business partnership.

Academic Routine and infrastructure

For many students, discipline is a great barrier, that is, the difficulty of organizing and executing their study schedules, making the academic routine a strong reason to choose traditional education, some people still have a barrier with technology, it may be because have not had de need to work with computers and the internet or because of the suspicion in them. If it is you or you know someone with these difficulties, I strongly recommend you to work on it or help someone to understand more about these issues to improve them, discipline and technology are skills that are extremely important for the job market.

It may be obvious, but the infrastructure is the main benefit that you are paying for the traditional one! It is what makes all the difference between online and traditional education, and it is also what ranking different colleges, with quality and price, making students achieve the best performance. It is not the name of the institution that will limit you to be a good professional, in the case your course requires expensive equipment, this is your best option and for those classes that do not need it, you still have the opportunity the study online if this is for you.

We are all self-taught Learners.

In a traditional education, a schedule of classes is already planned for the course, but the time of the classes is short, this makes the lectures ask to the students for academic reasearch and extra shoolwork,so that they can complete the grade and for that reason we must study outside the classroom, because just the classes are not enough. In addition, most of the time, the class schedule does not contain all the subject we need to go beyond the basics. In both ways we are running after the knowledge and we are not limited to the lecture´s PowerPoint.

Therefore, do not think that you are paying for knowledge when you are enrolling in a traditional education, you are only paying for the benefits mentioned above, it is up to you, self-taught, to pursue knowledge.
