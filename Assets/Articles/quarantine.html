Taking Advantage of Quarantine

By Adonides Amparo
March 31st, 2021

With the current situation in the world, we must take the opportunity to keep up with our studies. This is the right time to start to study if you have not started yet!

We are not question the decision of many countries to do or not the lockdown, this is March 2021 It means is one year after all those started around the globe and living with restriction and even total lockdown are driving us crazy.

Keep our mind busy might be one good solution to pass the time and avoid depression for this situation, I highly recommend online courses! It does not need to be the paid ones, there are so many good contents online from different points of views and languages.

One year ago, when all this started, I spend my quarantine studying Spanish, practicing meditation, learning guitar and exercising with push ups and abs. those were one month and a half of so productive and good moments, guess what I was feeling great!

Quarantine helps all that focus to happen, the true is in my head I had nothing else to do, because everything was closed, no restaurants, no bars, no meet ups some places that I used to go, I just met few was colleagues in my apartment time to time for dinner and drinks and games. I had everything under control, and everything happened under my wish, so study was much easier because that was no trip to be made other party to be at, there was not distraction in my mind to disturb my productive routine."

However, when the second wave came in January in this year, I was not able to keep up like before and why is that?

In February I realized what I need to bring back that good routine, with productive and joy moments… The good vibe around makes all the difference, yeah “happiness is the key for happiness”. I had lost the control and worries and express were taking over my head! So, I packed my luggage and went back to Brazil to my family, I needed to change the atmosphere, and be surrounded with close people with no worries about any next payment or of being unemployed for example.

It was a smart move, today I feel under control again, with productive and enjoying good times. My routines the study and projects are getting out to reality. I am happy and I want you to feel this happiness with yourself as well!!!
