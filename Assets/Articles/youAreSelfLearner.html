You are a Self-learner!!!

By Adonides Amparo
May 05th, 2021

Anyone is self-learner and should practice more of it, even who study in the traditional education. You may think you are not but, you will rethink about that for now on…

What is self-learner?

Many dictionaries say that, it is a person who learn something by herself, without lecture or just who learns a certain subject alone. I believe it is too vague!

Self-learner is when a person learns one subject by running after that knowledge in different methods, research, and a lot of practice till to obtain the knowledge by herself. And it does not mean that self-learner cannot ask helper to another person or even a lecture.

The responsibility of learning

Lectures will not teach you, but only guide you to the way, it means all responsibility is yours! Students must go after the knowledge in books, doing research, watching videos, and going to classes, with a lecture in class you will have someone to take away your doubt, and trying to simplify one explanation for you, but attention they do not know everything.

In class with a lecture, you can easily understand a certain subject, or in some situation not exactly, because we are all different, so you may not get the lectures explanation as the rest of your classmates would get and it is ok! You need to ask to another explanation in one better way for you to get the concept, and later you can search, even watch a video with other lecture on YouTube, just about the same theme from the class, thus understanding the theme clearly.

You are the one who want to learn, so you are responsible of your learning process, it is not the responsibility of the institution or of the lectures hold your hand daily asking for you to do your homework.

Being self-learner

It is impossible in the classes to have the time to discuss, practice, search and pass to all content planned to the semester, but in most of the time the institution cannot give the class with all the details the subject deserve. The lectures give assignments and exams with themes they never spoke in class for the lack of time.

For this is so important to understand yourself well, to help your process of learning to be easier, because only the content from the traditional education will be not enough for you to learn. You must have more sources of learning, filter the best methods for you and consuming as much you can, to increase your skills of research.

As we saw above it is necessary to study outside the classes, what have been discussed in class, it is the natural way to learn, practicing by yourself, so even after class you must manage your time to study at home, practicing what have learned in the class.
