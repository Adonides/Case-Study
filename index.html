The Future of Education

By Adonides Amparo
March 22, 2021

Before we enroll in college or an online course, we need to think about what is the best method to learn X, [Y] or Z.

Is the traditional education path the best option? and what should you think of online learning? In this blog we from StayFocus will run over through those questions and give our sincere opinion about it.

Traditional Education

The more I think about learning, the less excited I become about traditional learning. If you ask me, it´s about following a certain pattern, a routine and most of the time you´re forced to learn things you're not really interested in. And worse, that won't be of any use to you at your career path. You will not only have to invest a lot of money, but also a lot of time to constantly get from one place of learning to another.

The strongest defend to traditional learning would be the network, programs and internships offered from those institutes who has the power to partnership with companies. However, these benefits are not equally available to all alumni, but are only provided to a small group of students. No really, the main aspect in favor to traditional education I think is the structure, that´s because there are some subjects, career we must use expensives equipments, a very clear example is the studying of medicine, I would say it is almost impossible to buy a private lab. But maybe a half-time online course would to the job!

Online Learning

It can be so powerful, all we need to achieve that is a routine, like we do on the traditional learning, yeah exactly to have a specific time to study, manage the time could be the main point you will fail to have a success experience with online course.

That´s the kind of attitude I like. The point you push yourself to a new level! Because you are starting to become the boss of your learning, managing your time and being more independent, in some online courses you got classes on video and even you may interact with the lecture. The cool thing is you are managing your own flexible timetable, paying much less and you still have some support of the institution.

Happiness is the key to happiness.

The focus here in the end the day is to achieve our goal as soon as possible, and all the various methods of education there are pros and cons, and more than that changes for each person depending on how they apply them. In the way to potential your education we got know ourselves well to do it with happiness because believe does not matter you love the subject or not, the true is, hard times will come. It can be to understand one concert or lack of time to study, you might be frustrated then and be happy in what we are doing will surpass any frustration, because each hard time will be just a good challenge!
